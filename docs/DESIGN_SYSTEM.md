## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- **Red (SBB Red)**: `#DC143C` - Primary brand color
- **Black**: `#000000` - Text and accents
- **White**: `#FFFFFF` - Background and contrast

### Secondary Colors
- **Light Gray**: `#F5F5F5` - Backgrounds
- **Medium Gray**: `#E0E0E0` - Borders
- **Dark Gray**: `#666666` - Secondary text
- **Light Red**: `#FFF5F5` - Info backgrounds

## Typography

- **Font Family**: System fonts (Arial, Segoe UI, Roboto)
- **Headings**: Bold, 48-52px
- **Body**: Regular, 14-16px
- **Labels**: Semi-bold, 16px

## Components

### Buttons
- **Primary**: Red (`#DC143C`) with white text
- **Secondary**: Black (`#000000`) with white text
- **Hover**: Darker shade with shadow
- **Disabled**: Gray (`#CCCCCC`)

### Cards
- **Background**: White
- **Border**: Light gray (`#E0E0E0`)
- **Shadow**: Subtle drop shadow
- **Border Radius**: 12px

### Inputs
- **Border**: 2px solid gray
- **Focus**: Red border with shadow
- **Border Radius**: 8px

## SBB Logo

- **Location**: `/public/sbb-logo.svg`
- **Size**: 50px height
- **Style**: Red square with white "SBB" text
- **Usage**: Next to AZKT title in header

## Design Principles

1. **Clean & Minimal**: White backgrounds, clear hierarchy
2. **SBB Branding**: Red as primary color
3. **Professional**: Modern, trustworthy appearance
4. **Accessible**: High contrast, readable fonts
5. **Responsive**: Works on all screen sizes

## Implementation

All styles follow the red-white-black theme:
- Headers: Red text
- Buttons: Red or black
- Cards: White with gray borders
- Text: Black on white
- Accents: Red highlights
